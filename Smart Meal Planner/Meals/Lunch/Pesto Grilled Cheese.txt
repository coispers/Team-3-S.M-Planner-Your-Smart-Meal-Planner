8 slices sourdough bread
¼ cup butter (softened)
¼ cup pesto
2 tomatoes (sliced)
¼ cup Parmesan cheese (shredded)
8 slices Gouda cheese
INSTRUCTIONS
 
Butter one side of each slice of bread.

8 slices sourdough bread,¼ cup butter

Place 4 buttered slices on a cutting board or baking sheet with the buttered side down.
 
Spread pesto onto each of the 4 slices. Add tomatoes, Parmesan and Gouda cheeses. Place remaining bread on top with buttered side up.
¼ cup pesto,2 tomatoes,¼ cup Parmesan cheese,8 slices Gouda cheese

Preheat a griddle or a cast iron skillet on medium heat. Place 1-2 sandwiches on griddle. Cook for 2-3 minutes until bottom bread is nicely browned. 

Flip sandwiches and continue cooking for 2-3 more minutes until other side is brown and cheese is melted throughout.  Repeat with remaining sandwiches.
