Ingredients:

1 cup cottage cheese
Assorted fresh fruits (such as berries, sliced peaches, diced pineapple, or sliced bananas)
Optional toppings: honey, cinnamon, chopped nuts, or granola
Instructions:

Place the cottage cheese in a bowl or onto a serving plate.

Wash and prepare your choice of fresh fruits. Slice or dice them as desired.

Arrange the fresh fruits on top of the cottage cheese.

If desired, drizzle a small amount of honey over the fruit and cottage cheese for added sweetness.

Optional: Sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon or add chopped nuts or granola on top for added flavor and crunch.

Serve the cottage cheese with fruit immediately and enjoy!
