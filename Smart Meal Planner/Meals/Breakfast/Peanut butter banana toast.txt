Ingredients:

2 slices of bread (sourdough, whole wheat, or your preferred bread)
2 tablespoons peanut butter (smooth or crunchy)
1 ripe banana, sliced
Optional toppings: honey, cinnamon, chia seeds, or sliced almonds
Instructions:

Toast the slices of bread until golden brown.

Spread a layer of peanut butter on each slice of toasted bread.

Arrange the banana slices on top of the peanut butter, evenly distributing them.

Optional: Drizzle a small amount of honey over the banana slices for added sweetness.

Sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon, chia seeds, or sliced almonds on top for extra flavor and texture.

Serve the peanut butter banana toast immediately.
