Ingredients

1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
1 scoop of protein powder (your choice of flavor)
1 ripe banana
12 cup Greek yogurt
1 tablespoon nut butter (such as almond butter or peanut butter)
1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for added sweetness)
12 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup of ice cubes

Instructions

In a blender, add the milk, protein powder, ripe banana, Greek yogurt, nut butter, honey or maple syrup (if using), and vanilla extract.

Blend the ingredients on high speed until smooth and creamy.

Add the ice cubes to the blender and continue blending until the mixture is chilled and frothy.

Taste the smoothie and adjust the sweetness or thickness as desired by adding more honeymaple syrup or milk.

Pour the protein smoothie into a glass and serve immediately.
