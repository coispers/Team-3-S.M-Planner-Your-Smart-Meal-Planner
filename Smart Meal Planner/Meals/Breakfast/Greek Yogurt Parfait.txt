Ingredients:

1 cup Greek yogurt
1/2 cup granola
1 cup mixed fresh berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup
Optional toppings: nuts, seeds, shredded coconut, or additional fruits
Instructions:

In a bowl or glass, start by adding a layer of Greek yogurt at the bottom.

Top the yogurt layer with a layer of granola, spreading it evenly.

Add a layer of mixed fresh berries on top of the granola.

Drizzle some honey or maple syrup over the berries to add sweetness.

Repeat the layers until you use up all the ingredients, ending with a layer of berries on top.

For added crunch and flavor, you can sprinkle some nuts, seeds, shredded coconut, or additional fruits on the final layer.

Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing Greek yogurt parfait!
